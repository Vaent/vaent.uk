CardPeeGee can be played at home with nothing more than a pack of cards, and something to write on.

On this page are PDF files of the rules, which you can view in your browser, or download (right-click on the link and Save).


The full rules contain plenty of options for experienced players, who may want more of a challenge or just variations on style.


CardPeeGee 'Vanilla' is a stripped-down version, with most of the options left out to aid learning. The video game is based on this ruleset.
